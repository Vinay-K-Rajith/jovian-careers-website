About Jovian

Jovian is an online education platform that offers courses and programs in data science, machine learning, web development, and other fields. Jovian also provides a cloud-based environment for running and sharing Jupyter notebooks, as well as a community of learners and experts. Jovian has various job opportunities for people who are passionate about teaching, learning, and building software.
